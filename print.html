Frequently asked

Questions we hear before the first meeting.

Still have something we didn’t cover? Call us at (408) 555‑0142 or send a message — we’ll get back the same day.

What types of projects do you take on? +

Kitchen and bathroom remodels, ADUs and garage conversions, full‑home renovations, room additions, custom cabinetry, and mechanical work (HVAC + plumbing). Our typical project size runs from $40k bathrooms to $1M+ whole‑home remodels.

Do you provide free estimates? +

Yes — initial consultations and ballpark estimates are always free. For detailed, line‑item estimates on larger projects we may charge a refundable planning fee that goes toward construction.

Do you handle permits? +

Yes. We coordinate drawings, engineering where needed, submittals and inspections with the city. You won’t need to stand in a permit counter line.

How long does a typical remodel take? +

Bathrooms run 3–6 weeks. Kitchens 6–12 weeks. ADUs 4–7 months from permits to keys. Every estimate includes a realistic schedule — not an optimistic one.

How do you handle changes during the project? +

Every change is written up, priced and signed before work starts. No verbal agreements, no end‑of‑project surprises.

Are you licensed and insured? +

Yes. California CSLB license #1098421. We carry general liability and workers’ compensation insurance. We’re happy to send certificates before you sign.

Which areas do you serve? +

Palo Alto, Mountain View, Los Altos, Sunnyvale, Cupertino, San Jose, Santa Clara, Saratoga, Los Gatos, Redwood City, Menlo Park and surrounding Bay Area cities.
